Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as computational load?

Computational load includes financial scenarios, optimization problems, sensitivity analyses, Monte Carlo simulations, and usage of quantum backends or LLMs. More complex analyses that require quantum hardware or heavy AI processing contribute more to your usage.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle. Pro tier users are automatically scaled based on usage within the $2,000-$5,000 range.

Do you offer annual discounts?

Yes, annual subscriptions receive a 20% discount. Enterprise customers can negotiate custom annual agreements with additional benefits and volume discounts.

What kind of support is included?

Starter includes standard email support. Pro includes priority support with faster response times. Enterprise includes white-glove support, dedicated account management, and custom training sessions.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all tiers. This includes full access to the platform with usage limits similar to the Starter plan. No credit card required to start your trial.
